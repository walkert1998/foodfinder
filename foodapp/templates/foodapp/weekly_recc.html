The Royal With Cheese

by Cornelius Smithoctober 30,2018

Shoutouts for the week

  • Emmily Chapman
  • Ron Swanson
  • Chad Boudreu
  • Raymond Smith

Hello to all my readers this week! 

I know you all have patiently waited my spooktacular halloween themed weekly reccomendation for what to eat in wolfville. I know I know so was I, but I've listened to my roomate drone on and on about the royal with cheese at The Naked Crepe for weeks on end. Thus I finally caved and gave the royal with cheese a fair shot and man oh man what a crepe. What a crepe indeed!  That ooey gooey cheese and beef blend make you think you're the hamburglar himself. The royal with cheese is what a big mac and a whopper would be if they were cooked with love and weren't prone to giving you an upset tummy. Now I know what you're thinking with such a grandious name like the royal with cheese thats surely out of my budget, but heres the thing a regular costs only $8.75! This crepe has it ll going on and thats why it even beat out those spoopy halloween themed donuts at Tims for my reccomendation this week.
